About Palm Oasis Trading

Your trusted partner in premium teak wood supply since 2013

Our Sourcing Network

Latin America

We source premium teak from Brazil, Ecuador, Panama, Costa Rica, and Guatemala. These regions are renowned for their mature teak plantations that produce dense, high-oil-content wood ideal for processing.

West Africa

Our African sourcing includes Ghana, Togo, and Benin, where we work exclusively with licensed suppliers who operate under regulated forestry practices and sustainable management principles.

Canada

We source high-quality Canadian pine from the dense, sustainably managed forests of British Columbia, Alberta, and Ontario. These regions are known for their cold climates and slow-growing pine trees, which result in strong, straight-grained wood with excellent workability and a consistent textureâ€”perfect for construction, furniture, and finishing applications.

Sustainable Practices

All sourcing is conducted with strict attention to sustainability standards, origin verification, and species authenticity. We maintain long-term supplier relationships that ensure reliable, scalable operations suited to the sawmilling industry's operational timelines.
